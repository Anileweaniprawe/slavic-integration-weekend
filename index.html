WHAT IS INTEGRATION WEEKEND?

Integration Weekend is a three-day trip where your Erasmus really begins.

We’ve been organising Integration Weekends since 2010 - and this year’s Slavic edition is our 29th. Over the years, we’ve welcomed hundreds of international students and helped them start their semester in the best possible way!

It’s all about meeting new people, building friendships and feeling part of an international community from the beginning of your Erasmus. Through team activities, creative workshops, themed moments and a lot of shared laughs, you’ll connect with students from all over the world in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere.

This time, we’ll let you discover our Slavic roots, challenge a few stereotypes and show you what Poland is really about.

ORGANISER

ESN-EYE Łódź (Erasmus Student Network - European Youth Exchange) is a non-profit student organisation dedicated to fostering cultural exchange and integration through a variety of projects for both international and local student communities. Based at Lodz University of Technology, we are part of the broader Erasmus Student Network, which spans 44 countries and includes over 515 local sections with more than 15 000 volunteers.
